Information @ your Desktop


Welcome! Here is the port of information that you need from the university.

Whether your academic performance details, time table, exam time table, earned credits, financial details or communicating with university, You can access your University account 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Just log on to your account with the Register number as user name and date of birth as password at first time and you are ready to go.

First time User? [You must set your password]

If you are a Student and this is your first login, you must use your Register number/StudentID(you may get it from your admission letter and receipt), with your date of birth [Format ddmmyyyy E.g. 03121990]

If you are a Registered user, please enter your Register number/StudentID and password to login.
